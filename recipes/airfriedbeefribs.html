No Smoke from Those Charcoals

Description

This recipe is for cooking "fall off the bone"beef ribs in that pretty, fancy air fryer with all the buttons (Don't feel judged. The air fryer accounts for half of our power bill.)

Season those ribs and pop them in the air fryer. Let them cook while watching that new Sonic movie your kids have been talking about.

Let's get to it.

Ingredients

  • 1 rack of Beef Short Ribs
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of your favorite bbq rub - just to ensure that they have plenty of flavor
  • 1/2 cup of bbq sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil

Steps

  1. Rinse the ribs
  2. Pat dry with paper towels
  3. Peel off the silver membrane from the back of the ribs, using a bonig knife.
  4. Apply olive oil to the ribs
  5. Apply all of the seasoning, both sides.
  6. Preheat your air fryer to 320 degrees, then pop the short ribs in meat side up for 25 minutes.
  7. Once the timer sounds, flip them over and cook at 320 for another 5-10 minutes.

At this point, if you have sides bring them to the table.

If not, make sure to get a rib in those hands and show them whose house it is.
